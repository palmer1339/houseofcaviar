CAVIAR LIFE STYLE

CAVIAR

 

Caviar is a first rate menswear label Designed in Boston and Made in New England.

 

Our process is aimed at well made, fine and responsibly sourced fabrics to ensure first class standards.

 

Also, our skillful design process produces exceptional clothing, sportswear, collection goods and accessories that harmonize well with each other.

 

Furthermore, Caviar products gain added value over time because it’s American Made as well as manufactured in small unit-numbered batches that run on a limited basis coupled alongside sophisticated innovation.

 

Founder of Boston-based House Of Caviar, R.L. Paul is a man on a mission to providing an authentic twist on luxury. His Entrepreneurial spirit, eye for details and focus of vision serve as examples of the passion that is exuded through Caviar.
